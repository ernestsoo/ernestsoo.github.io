Our Mission

Powerlifting Kuching aims to provide a platform for all athletes of different backgrounds to showcase their strength in the sport of Powerlifting.

The mission of Powerlifting Kuching is to promote the health and mental benefits of strength training and the sport of Powerlifting in Malaysia, particularly East Malaysia.

Powerlifting Kuching strives to provide recognition to all strength athletes of all levels through hosting powerlifting competitions regularly.
